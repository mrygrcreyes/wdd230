All tourism stakeholders in Region 4A!

The TIEZA - Tourism Infrastructure and Enterprise Zone Authority, in partnership with the Department of Tourism Calabarzon Office and Philippine Chamber of Commerce and Industry (PCCI) Rizal and Antipolo, is inviting you to the investment promotion roadshow entitled, "𝐓𝐈𝐄𝐙𝐀 𝐈𝐧𝐯𝐞𝐬𝐭𝐦𝐞𝐧𝐭 𝐈𝐧𝐜𝐞𝐧𝐭𝐢𝐯𝐞𝐬 &𝐎𝐩𝐩𝐨𝐫𝐭𝐮𝐧𝐢𝐭𝐢𝐞𝐬 𝐟𝐨𝐫 𝐓𝐨𝐮𝐫𝐢𝐬𝐦 𝐄𝐧𝐭𝐞𝐫𝐩𝐫𝐢𝐬𝐞𝐬," on 08 February 2024, 9:30 am - 3:00 pm at the First Pacific Leadership Academy in Antipolo City,Rizal. This roadshow aims to raise awareness and update the tourism stakeholders regarding Corporate Recovery and Tax Incentives for Enterprises (CREATE) Act and its incentive package; promote the assistance programs of DOT and TIEZA; encourage investors to register their tourism projects with TIEZA; offer investment opportunities to tourism stakeholders; and introduce financial institutions that can offer loan grants to investors. REGISTER NOW at: https://bit.ly/TIEZAInvestRoadshowFeb8
Thank you and we hope to see you there!
