Rizal Chamber of Commerce

Site Plan for the Chamber of Commerce Website

Mary Grace Reyes - WDD 230 Web Frontend Development

Overview

The Chamber of Commerce of Rizal is name represents a city in which the chamber operates. It is a local organization of businesses in Rizal with the purpose of growing and advancing the interests of local businesses in the Philippines. Many businesses are multinational corporations that have offices in the Philippines.

Purpose

The Chamber of Commerce Rizal website would act as a one-stop online shop for all nearby companies and people living in the Rizal area. Our goal is to promote economic growth by prioritizing the development of local entrepreneurs and advocating for profitable business laws and concepts in our local area. We provide a variety of services as a chamber of commerce, including organizing networking gatherings, raising funds to assist small businesses, establishing an environment friendly to learning opportunities, and a lot more. By uniting local businesses, creating connections between them, and having community involvement at its core, we can create an effective local economy. When we work as an entire family, we can create a more resilient and successful community that benefits everybody.
